MOQs & Timing

Some items require a supplier MOQ (minimum order quantity). If your request doesn’t meet MOQ, you’ll have options:

  • Group Order / Pre-Order: I’ll combine requests and update you on timing.
  • Add Items: Add a few items to start immediately.
  • Small Surcharge: Cover a small balance so I can move ahead solo.
  • My Discretion: If demand is high, I may purchase the remaining units myself and keep them in local stock.

I’ll be transparent about timing so you can choose what works best.
